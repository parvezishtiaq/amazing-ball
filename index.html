Balls in Sports, Games, and Leisure

A ball is a spherical object that can be used for various purposes such as sports, games, and toys. It is typically made of rubber, plastic, or leather. They can be kicked, thrown, bounced, or hit with a bat or racket. Balls are an essential part of many activities and have been around for centuries.
